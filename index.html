Bonjour! I'm Dreyke Boone.

Software Development graduate, Technical Product Analyst at Maximus, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu student, and Arizona State University student.

I have a passion leading cross-disciplinary teams to innovate and deliver!

This site will guide you through some of my favorite things, from music to travel to books. Enjoy!

Music

Music has always been a large part of my life. From trumpet to bass to synthesizers, manipulating sound is a fantastic experience.

I used to be obsessed with effect pedals, at one time having about 16 effect pedals being circulated from my pedal board.

I try to keep a wide taste of music though I’ve noticed a tendency to list to jazz-influenced artists. Favorite artists include: Hiatus Kaiyote, Sampha, Flying Lotus, Thundercat, Kamasi Washington, City and Colour, and Frank Ocean.

I’ve recently picked up playing acoustic guitar which has been a fun experience!

Traveling

While I’ve been to many countries, I have not been to enough. From Amsterdam to Canada and now adding Thailand to the list in April.

There’s something about immersing one’s self with a foreign culture. It forces new ways of thinking, if allowed.

Not to mention, the variety of food. There’s just…. So much good food to enough in life.

Books books books!

I’ve either got my nose glued to a computer screen or a book. Sometimes both. There’s just so much knowledge to absorb and not enough hours of the day.

Some of my favorite books include:

Thinking Fast and Slow by Daniel Kahneman, Maps of Meaning by Jordan Peterson, Race Work & Leadership by Laura Morgan Roberts, Anthony J. Mayo, and David A. Thomas, and Thinking in Systems by Donelle H. Meadows.
